## Gateway 6: Creating Your Digital Presence

**Theme**: Building Your Website with Lovable

### Objectives
- Generate website prompt from your Tana blueprint
- Create first version of personal website using Lovable
- Learn to update and customize your site
- Establish your authentic digital presence

### Core Teachings

#### Why Your Website Matters

**Your Blueprint as Website Foundation**
- Your cosmic design informs your brand, message, and offering
- Authentic presence attracts aligned opportunities
- Your website is your digital temple, reflecting your essence
- Technology as extension of your purpose

**What Your Website Can Include**:
- About/Bio page rooted in your blueprint
- Your wisdom traditions and how you work with them
- Offerings or services aligned with your design
- Blog or insights sharing your journey
- Contact/connection points

#### The Tana-to-Lovable Bridge

**How It Works**:
1. Inside your Tana workspace, you'll find a `#[[Website Generator]]` supertag
2. Fill in fields with information about:
   - Your story and background
   - Your cosmic blueprint highlights
   - Your offerings/services/focus areas
   - Your brand voice and visual preferences
   - Pages you want on your site
3. Use the custom AI command: `/generate-website-prompt`
4. This produces a comprehensive prompt for Lovable based on:
   - Your personal context
   - Your cosmic blueprint synthesis
   - Your stated website goals
   - Your brand preferences

**The Generated Prompt Includes**:
- Site structure and pages
- Content themes drawn from your blueprint
- Design aesthetic aligned with your chart (e.g., Leo rising → bold, radiant design)
- Functionality requirements
- Brand voice guidelines

#### Using Lovable to Build Your Site

**Step-by-Step Process**:
1. Copy the generated prompt from Tana
2. Go to lovable.dev and start a new project
3. Paste the prompt and let Lovable generate first version
4. Review the generated site structure

**Making Changes and Updates**:
- **Content Updates**: Tell Lovable "Update the About page to say [new content]"
- **Design Changes**: "Make the color scheme more [earthy/cosmic/minimalist]"
- **New Pages**: "Add a Services page with [specific offerings]"
- **Layout Adjustments**: "Move the testimonials section below the hero"
- **Component Updates**: "Change the contact form to include [specific fields]"

**Lovable Best Practices**:
- Be specific with your requests
- Make iterative changes (one thing at a time)
- Review each change before requesting the next
- Use your blueprint themes to guide design decisions
- Ask Lovable to explain options if you're unsure

#### Keeping Website Aligned with Evolution

**Your Blueprint Evolves, So Should Your Site**:
- Update quarterly as you deepen into your design
- Reflect new offerings or focus areas
- Share insights from your journey
- Adjust based on transits, personal year changes, Gene Keys contemplations

**Maintaining the Tana-Website Connection**:
- Keep your `#[[Website Generator]]` fields updated in Tana
- Regenerate prompts when making major updates
- Use AI to draft new content based on recent blueprint insights

### Process
1. Complete `#[[Website Generator]]` fields in Tana
2. Run `/generate-website-prompt` command
3. Copy output and create new Lovable project
4. Review initial website generation
5. Make 3-5 customization requests to refine
6. Update surface-level placeholder content with real information
7. Publish your site

### Deliverables
- Generated website prompt from Tana
- Live website created in Lovable
- Understanding of how to update and maintain site
